Research Interests

My current goals are to use cutting-edge imaging, sequencing, and biochemical techniques alongside computational and statistical learning methods to advance basic science and therapeutics.

Broad Goals

  • Noncoding RNAs: How are they chemically and quantitatively regulated? Can their structure predict function? How do they transmit epigenetic memory across generations?
  • Gene regulation: How is chromatin organized in 3D space in the nucleus?
  • Immunology: What causes autoimmune diseases, such as lupus? What is the weakest link in the HIV reproductive cycle, and how can we leverage it to develop therapeutics?
